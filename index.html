About Us

AlumniTalks is a student-founded organization that supports teenagers through the high school years, addressing common but often unspoken concerns. Our mission is to bridge the gap between high school and higher education by connecting students with university students and graduates within or out of the Peel Region. We provide valuable insights and support to help students navigate their future with confidence.
